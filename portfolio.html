(Fish) Brain Computer Interface

For 11 weeks at Backyard Brains, I developed a neuroethological research tool for weakly-electric fish. These fish are cool because they emit sinusoidal bioelectric signals. This made it easy to mimic and manipulate the signals produced by these fish.
